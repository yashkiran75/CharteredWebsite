Audit & Assurance:

With globalization, accounting rules, regulations and audit standards are changing very frequently. Our audit procedures are designed to ensure compliance with relevant statutory laws and specialization. Our approach focuses on ensuring that internal controls are adequate and disclosure requirements of the accounting standards and applicable regulations are met with. Audits can be expected to detect flaws in the system, but due to constraints, an audit can only manage to provide reasonable assurance that the statements in the audit report are free from error—a concept influenced by both qualitative and quantitative factors. Auditing is not just a vital part of accounting but also a legal requirement. Our trained team of experts effectively analyses and improves the control and governance of a system, complying with all your auditing needs. List of services we provide under this section are:



Statutory Audit

Audit under the provisions of the Companies Act, Insurance Act, Banking Regulation Act, Co-operative Societies Act of banks both nationalized as well as co-operative, public as well as private limited companies, partnership firms and proprietary concerns.

Tax Audit

Audit under the provisions of the Income-Tax Act for compliance with the provisions of the act and allow ability or otherwise of deductions/ exemptions in various cases like exporters, new industrial undertakings and charitable institutions.

Stock Audit

Carried out stock audit of borrowers of various branches of nationalized banks for ascertaining the value of stock and book debts, drawing power, end use of fund and primary and secondary security.

Concurrent Audit

Performed concurrent audit of branches of various nationalized banks and co-operative banks for verifying the compliance with the directions of RBI and head office.

Internal Audit

-Involves verification of internal control system, checking of data generated in ERP, vouching of purchases, sales, import, export, banks, cash, branch transactions, bank reconciliation, excise records, VAT/GST records, production planning, stock records, physical verification of stock, ledger scrutiny, human resource records, etc. of companies.

Goods and Servicestax Audit:

  • Checking and verification of Goods and Servicestax audit file prepared by tax assistant along with profile of the clients.
  • Client visit at the time of audit and discussion of the same with audit parties. Solutions of audit issues, justification for para arising during the finalization of audit report and modus operandi issues arising during the audit.

Certification:

Certificates for availing disbursements of term loans from banks/financial institutions, certifying creation of assets and details of sources of funds, export and inland turnover for licence purposes, cost of production of particular product and various certificates in connection with raising of public money.
